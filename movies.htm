Best Stand-Alone Movie - V for Vendetta

This dystopian political action film is adapted from the 1988 DC comic short series of the same name. It is set in London in the near-future where a Fascist Totalitarian government reigns. The two main characters (one being the masked freedom fighter known as "V") attempt to ignite a righteous revolution.

**Special shout-outs to My Neighbor Totoro, Laputa - Castle in the Sky, and Monty Python & the Holy Grail**

Best Movie Series - The Lord of the Rings

Peter Jackson's adaptations of the iconic Tolkien series was a massive hit in the early 2000's that still hold up today. The trilogy won 17 Academy Awards and the 3rd movie "Return of the King" is tied for the most Academy Awards in a single movie (11).

**Special shout-outs to Star Wars, the Marvel Cinimatic Universe, and Bluey**

Best TV Series - Parks and Recreation

This work-place comedy ran from 2009-2015. "The absurd antics of an Indiana town's public officials as they pursue sundry projects to make their city a better place."

**Special shout-outs to Ink Master, Lost, and the Magicians**
